Quizzify

An app which lets teachers assign quizzes to students easier than ever!

Info

This app is made by @somePythonProgrammer, to make online assignments easier.

About the app

This app has a leaderboard, points system, badges and achievements to make it exciting for students. It's pretty easy to make and send the assignments to students too! Students can't cheat, there's an anti-cheating system!

Features

Easy for teachers

It's pretty easy for teachers to make assignments here!

Anti Cheat

Teachers don't have to worry about cheating, as there's an anti-cheat system!

Exciting for students

There are points, a leaderboard, badges and achievements to make the student enjoy while learning! After the quiz is over, the app explains the wrong answer to the students automatically!

Email notifications

Students and teachers get email updates every hour!
